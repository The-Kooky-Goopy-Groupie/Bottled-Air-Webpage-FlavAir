Our Mission

Established in Lakeland, Florida in 1990, our goal has still remained relevant: provide people with consistent access to clean, natural air. While we have undergone some changes to ensure the best experience for our consumers, such as air flavoring and nutrient addition, at our core we have remained the same.

Though we started as a small business with an uncertain future, today we have expanded to include several locations throughout Florida, also providing access to our products through an online shop to allow anyone, anywhere, to try our air.
